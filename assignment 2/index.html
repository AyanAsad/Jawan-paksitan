ESSAY: WHY TO BOYCOTT ISRAEL PRODUCTS

Boycotting Israeli products is a way some people show their disagreement with what they see as human rights issues in the Israeli-Palestinian conflict. They believe that by not buying Israeli goods, they can put pressure on the Israel govt to change its actions.
However, others worry that this boycott could harm innocent Israeli businesses and make it harder to find peace. It's important to understand both sides before deciding whether to boycott Israeli products. Being informed about the situation and the potential impacts of a boycott is crucial in making a thoughtful decision.
